✦ FAQ & CLEAR BOUNDARIES ✦

Clear boundaries are part of honest service.

NOVI explains what is included, what can be added later, and what is outside the project scope.

Do you edit existing WordPress, Wix, Squarespace, or Shopify sites?

No. NOVI does not edit or fix existing platform-based websites.

Do you build e-commerce or Shopify stores?

No. NOVI focuses on service-based small businesses and lightweight one-page foundation websites.

Is ongoing SEO or advertising included?

No. NOVI does not sell ongoing SEO, ad management, or monthly operations as the basic package.

Can analysis or A/B testing be added later?

Yes. Deeper analysis, reports, and A/B testing can be quoted later when there is enough traffic and a clear goal.

Are PageSpeed or SEO scores guaranteed?

No ranking or inquiry results are guaranteed. NOVI builds with PageSpeed, accessibility, SEO, and clean structure in mind.

Who owns the domain and accounts?

The client should own their domain, Google accounts, and business profiles.
